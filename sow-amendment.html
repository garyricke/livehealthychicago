Statement of Work — Amendment No. 1

Live Healthy Chicago · livehealthychi.com

Prepared by Gary Ricke, Orbis Design
Prepared for Alexandra Sontag, DNP — Director of Community Initiatives, The University of Chicago Medicine
Date July 16, 2026
Amends The original Statement of Work for the Live Healthy Chicago website (total fee $25,000)

This amendment adds a pre-paid maintenance package to the original Statement of Work and defines the scope it covers. It does not change the terms of the original SOW; it follows it.

1. Purpose

The original SOW is complete and its budget is effectively exhausted. This amendment does two things:

  • Establishes a $5,000 pre-paid maintenance package to fund ongoing work on the site through the rest of the year.
  • Defines the Community Health Worker (CHW) training set for Ella Lawal’s project as a fixed-fee deliverable drawn from that package.

2. Completion of the Original SOW — No Additional Charge

The following items finish the original project and are delivered at no additional charge under the original SOW. They are listed here for clarity, not to be billed:

  • Portal go-live. Turning the provider/CHW resource portal on for live use, including a sign-ups administrative view — a simple, spreadsheet-style list of who has registered showing name, email, organization, and job title, filterable by job type (CHWs, providers, nurses) so content can be targeted to the right audience.
  • Portal training & update workflow. A short, shareable training or written workflow covering how the team loads resources and updates site content, so the work is not dependent on any one person.
  • Coalition-first hero. Publishing the new landing focus that presents Live Healthy Chicago as a complete idea rather than a blood-pressure-only initiative.

Any further work on the portal itself remains part of the original SOW and will not be billed against the maintenance package.

3. Scope of Services — Maintenance

The pre-paid package covers ongoing and net-new work, including:

  • Routine content edits. Event announcements, news items, copy and image updates. Most run roughly $50–$150 each, and less per item when batched together. Events continue to be requested by email, which is working well.
  • Brand guide content updates. As LHC’s communications consultant develops the brand guide further, updates flowing back into the site.
  • Messaging alignment. Consultation with LHC’s communications consultant to keep the site aligned with the coalition’s larger messaging.
  • CHW training set. Ella Lawal’s project, as defined in Section 4.
  • Ongoing site upkeep. Dependency and security updates, form and data plumbing, and general maintenance of the live site.

4. Scope of Services — CHW Training Set

A training set supporting Ella Lawal’s DNP quality-improvement project, produced by interviewing Ella on camera and letting AI amplify the human work — drafting the interview questions against her script, doing the research, designing the print pieces, and editing the audio and video. Deliverables:

  • A five-module CHW refresher (~32 minutes) in the chaptered format already built for the Nuna interview stories: (1) Understanding High Blood Pressure, (2) Preventing & Managing, (3) Know Your Numbers, (4) Recognizing an Emergency, (5) The CHW’s Role.
  • Short headlined clips pulled from each module for standalone use.
  • Six on-screen graphics packages supporting the script: the AHA blood-pressure category table, before/during measurement checklists, BE FAST, the >180/120 emergency decision flow, the CHW toolbox, and a QR resource card.
  • A tri-fold print pamphlet and a CHW community insert — professionally designed, on-brand, written at a 7th–9th grade reading level, with QR codes linking to the LHC site and the resource portal.
  • Publication to the LHC site.

Recording is a single session — no reshoots. Content and clinical guidance in the script have been reviewed and approved by LHC (Alexandra Sontag, July 14, 2026).

Timeline. The script is locked. Recording is scheduled for the week of August 3, 2026 (following Ella’s return on August 1), with delivery ahead of the August 25, 2026 CHW training.

Fee and overage cap. This set is billed at a fixed $1,500 (20 hours) drawn from the pre-paid package.

The script as written is larger than the ~15-minute refresher that figure was originally quoted against — the realistic effort for the deliverables above is approximately 33 hours (~$2,500). Orbis will absorb that difference at no charge, capped at $1,000 (~13 hours). LHC’s cost for this deliverable is $1,500 regardless. Should LHC later add scope beyond what is listed above, additional hours would draw from the package’s remaining balance with written approval — but nothing in the current script triggers that.
